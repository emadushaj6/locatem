About Locatem

Locatem is a SaaS-style platform focused on simplifying how users discover and interact with local businesses. The platform is designed to provide a structured, category-based system where services can be explored efficiently, while also offering businesses a clear and scalable way to present themselves online.

Platform Overview

Locatem operates as a centralized directory where businesses are organized into clearly defined categories and locations. This structure allows users to navigate services intuitively, avoiding the clutter and inconsistency often found in traditional search methods.

Each business listed on the platform includes essential information such as contact details, descriptions, and relevant media. This ensures that users can make informed decisions without needing to rely on multiple sources.

Core Functionality

  • Structured business profiles with essential information
  • Category-based navigation for simplified browsing
  • Location-based discovery for relevant results
  • Search and filtering capabilities
  • Scalable promotion system for business visibility

Approach and Philosophy

The platform is built with a strong emphasis on usability and clarity. Instead of overwhelming users with excessive data or complex interfaces, Locatem focuses on delivering a streamlined experience where information is easy to access and understand. From a business perspective, the goal is to provide a reliable digital presence that can grow over time. The platform is structured to support gradual enhancement through subscription-based visibility and feature access.

Subscription Model

Locatem includes a tiered subscription system designed to provide increasing levels of visibility and functionality. While the platform remains accessible at a basic level, businesses can choose to enhance their presence depending on their needs.

  • Basic: Entry-level listing with essential business information
  • Standard: Increased visibility with additional content and media support
  • Premium: Maximum exposure with priority placement and full profile capabilities

Technology Stack

Locatem is developed using a modern and efficient technology stack. The backend is powered by Django, providing a robust and scalable architecture. The frontend is built with HTML and CSS, focusing on clean and maintainable design patterns.

The current database solution is SQLite, primarily used during development for simplicity and speed. The architecture allows for seamless migration to more advanced database systems as the platform grows.

Development Status

The platform is currently in active development. Core features have been implemented, including business listing, category organization, and foundational UI components. Ongoing work includes improving search functionality, refining the user interface, and completing the subscription-based promotion system.

Future Direction

The long-term vision for Locatem is to evolve into a fully-featured platform that supports both users and businesses at scale. Planned improvements include enhanced filtering, better ranking algorithms, and more advanced tools for business management.


The focus remains on maintaining a balance between simplicity and functionality, ensuring that the platform remains accessible while continuing to grow in capability.
